## Visual direction
Before changing rendering, materials, lighting, camera, particles, or environment art, read `docs/art_direction.md`.

Prefer:
- silhouette clarity
- fog-based composition
- cheap geometry with strong lighting
- restrained palette
- whale readability over environment detail

Do not imitate reference games literally or copy assets/UI. Use references only as inspiration.
When making visual changes, explain briefly how they support atmosphere, readability, or mythic scale.
