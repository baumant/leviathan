# Whale Hero Concept

## Intent
The whale should read as a sperm whale immediately from the gameplay camera, but still hold up in closer side views. The silhouette stays broad, smooth, and mythic so fog and lighting carry the mood instead of fine texture noise.

## Locked Form Cues
- Forehead: large squared block with a blunt front plane.
- Jaw: slimmer underslung lower jaw that clearly separates from the forehead mass.
- Back: low hump with a knuckle ridge instead of a fin-like dorsal read.
- Fins: smaller lower-set pectorals so the head mass stays dominant.
- Tail: strong taper into the peduncle with wide, graphic flukes.
- Details: visible eye, blowhole, scars, and restrained surface breakup for closeups.

## Variants
- Player whale: bone white, brightest moving form, subtle cool underside separation.
- Captive whale: gray-blue, dimmer and more natural, same species silhouette.

## Style Boundaries
- Preserve smooth masses and readable silhouette over realism-heavy rendering.
- Keep materials stepped and fog-friendly.
- Avoid shiny PBR response, dense normal detail, or noisy textures that would weaken mythic scale.
